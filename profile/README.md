## About Us | Über uns

Based in Jena, Germany, Narrovosoft is a tech and education company at the intersection of innovation and learning. We combine cutting-edge technologies like AI and IoT with the timeless art of storytelling to create engaging educational experiences for learners of all ages.

Narrovosoft ist ein Technologie- und Bildungsunternehmen mit Sitz in Jena, Deutschland. Wir verbinden modernste Technologien wie KI und IoT mit der Kunst des Geschichtenerzählens, um inspirierende Lernerfahrungen für alle Altersgruppen zu schaffen.

---

## Narratelica

Our flagship product — a platform that sparks imagination through technology.

Unser Hauptprodukt — eine Plattform, die durch Technologie die Fantasie anregt.

📱 **Mobile App** — Children collect physical tokens, scan them with their phone, and AI generates unique, personalized stories. Every token unlocks a new adventure.

📚 **DIY STEM Kit** — A 9-book series for teens (9+) to build their own IoT storytelling device from scratch, learning electronics, coding, and engineering along the way.
